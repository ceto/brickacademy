How to Become an
Archviz Wizard

As one of the top architectural visualization companies, Brick Visual opens its doors to those who imagine their career in architectural visualization, but lacking the experience or technical knowledge.

With personalized, mentor-led practices and workshops, you will learn all the skills and techniques of image creation that are required from a 3D artist, such as:

  • fundamentals of art of ArchViz
  • professional 3Ds Max, VRay and Adobe Photoshop knowledge
  • killer composition and color management skills
  • hands-on knowledge and experience in real-life architectural visualization projects
  • Drafting and experimenting skills in image creation

By successfully completing the 3D Junior ArchViz course and exams, candidates may be offered a 3D artist position at Brick Visual.

Teachers & Mentors

Learn valuable knowledge from Brick Visual’s top industry professionals. Our mentors will only tutor 3-4 people. The tailor-made program encourages personal, immediate interaction and instant feedback during the course.

Office & Facilities

You will learn in our well-equipped, spacious and full of light office at Budapest and experience the easy-going vibe of our community. Brick Academy’s conscious teaching method gives you the unique chance to observe, how a rather big, high-profile visualization studio works with international, top architectural companies, such as BIG, Snøhetta or SOM.

High-tech Environment

As archviz requires reliable technical background, we offer well-equipped workstations, you can work effectively and smoothly. You will have full access to Brick Visual’s green box studio, cameras, professional lights and VR gadgets. Each workstation comes with powerful computers, necessary software and large Wacom Cintiqs.
