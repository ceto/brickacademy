How to Become an Archviz Wizard

As one of the top architectural visualization companies, Brick Visual opens its doors to those who imagine their career in architectural visualization, but lacking the experience or technical knowledge.

With personalized, mentor-led practices and workshops, you will learn all the skills and techniques of image creation that are required from a 3D artist, such as:

  • fundamentals of art of ArchViz
  • professional 3Ds Max, VRay and Adobe Photoshop knowledge
  • killer composition and color management skills
  • hands-on knowledge and experience in real-life architectural visualization projects
  • Drafting and experimenting skills in image creation

By successfully completing the 3D Junior ArchViz course and exams, candidates may be offered a 3D artist position at Brick Visual.
