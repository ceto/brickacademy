One week from
basics to editing

By completing this course, you will learn all the fundamental aspects of architectural visualization movie making; from finding the right source of inspiration to editing, post-production and beyond. This is for you if you have intermediate 3ds Max skills and basic movie editing knowledge.

  • You will learn in a professional environment, in the Brick HQ
  • Master exceptional composition and storytelling skills
  • Learn the essence of movie basics, camera settings, animation and shooting timelapse movies.
  • Introduction to editing and lighting
  • Create efficient workflows by acquiring render management skills and Post-production basics
  • Boost your Davinci Resolve, After Effects knowledge
